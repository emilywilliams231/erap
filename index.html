Available Assistance Programs

Rental Assistance (ERAP)

Financial relief for tenants struggling to pay rent or utilities due to economic hardship. Benefits include:

  • Up to 12 months of rental arrears
  • Utilities and home energy costs
  • Relocation assistance services

Home Equity (HELOC)

Leverage the equity in your property for a flexible line of credit. Ideal for homeowners seeking:

  • Debt consolidation at lower rates
  • Major home improvements/renovations
  • Emergency liquidity for large expenses
